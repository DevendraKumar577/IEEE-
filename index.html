About The Event

The IEEE MANIT Hackathon 2024 is an engaging, team-based coding event designed for students, professionals, and technology enthusiasts. This competition encourages participants to come together to brainstorm, innovate, and create solutions for practical challenges. Teams will develop prototypes and present their final projects to a panel of expert judges, showcasing their creativity and technical skills.
